# TCS Wins Multi-Year Deal with European Bank

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) announced a £350 million, five-year digital transformation deal with a leading European bank. The contract covers cloud migration, data platform modernization, and AI-driven customer service tools.

TCS reported a total contract value (TCV) of $13.2 billion in deal wins for FY2024, its highest ever. Revenue growth for FY2024 came in at 4.1% in constant currency, impacted by a slowdown in the BFSI and retail verticals in North America.

The company remains the most profitable large-cap IT firm in India with EBIT margins consistently above 24%. Headcount reduction of ~14,000 in FY2024 reflects automation-led efficiency gains.

TCS continues to be a core holding in most institutional portfolios, trading at a premium PE of 28x on a trailing basis.
