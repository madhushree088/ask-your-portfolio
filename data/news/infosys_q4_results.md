# Infosys Q4 Results Beat Estimates

Infosys reported strong Q4 FY2024 results, with revenue growing 6.4% year-on-year in constant currency terms. The IT major posted a net profit of ₹7,969 crore, slightly ahead of analyst expectations.

The company maintained its revenue growth guidance for FY2025 at 1–3% in constant currency, citing cautious client spending in the BFSI segment amid global macroeconomic uncertainty.

CEO Salil Parekh highlighted strong deal wins totalling $4.5 billion in Q4, indicating a healthy pipeline. Margins improved sequentially by 40 basis points to 20.7%, driven by operational efficiency and lower subcontracting costs.

Analysts remain cautiously optimistic. The stock has outperformed the Nifty IT index year-to-date, up approximately 12%.
