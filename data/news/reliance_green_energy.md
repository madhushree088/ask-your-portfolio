# Reliance Industries Expands Green Energy Push

Reliance Industries announced a ₹75,000 crore capital expenditure plan for its New Energy business over the next three years. The conglomerate is building gigafactories for solar panels, batteries, and green hydrogen at its Jamnagar complex in Gujarat.

The company's retail and Jio digital segments continue to drive consolidated revenue growth. Retail reported GMV of ₹3.8 lakh crore in FY2024, while Jio added 8 million subscribers in Q4 alone.

Oil-to-Chemicals (O2C) segment margins remained under pressure due to weak refining spreads globally. However, analysts expect recovery in H2 FY2025 as global demand picks up.

Reliance remains the largest company by market cap on Indian exchanges. The stock is seen as a defensive bet with diversified revenue streams.
