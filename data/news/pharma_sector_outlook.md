# Indian Pharma Sector Outlook FY2025

The Indian pharmaceutical sector is poised for a strong FY2025, driven by US generic drug approvals picking up pace and domestic formulation growth of 8–10%.

Sun Pharma, the largest pharma company by market cap in India, continues to outperform with its specialty product pipeline in the US gaining traction. Its Ilumya and Cequa brands reported double-digit growth in the US market.

Dr. Reddy's Laboratories is benefiting from gRevlimid exclusivity and has guided for continued US revenue momentum. The company is also scaling its biosimilars business in Europe.

Key risks for the sector include US FDA regulatory actions, pricing pressure in the US generic market, and rupee depreciation impact on raw material imports. Overall, analysts rate the sector as Overweight for FY2025.
